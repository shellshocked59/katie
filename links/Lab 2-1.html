Please Help!

Our annual food drive takes place this month. This event is the largest one-day food drive in the metropolition area and has helped feed those in need since 1991. We strive to provide the most nutritious food for the individuals and families in our community. If you are unable to bring items to the food bank at 2828 N. Central Avenue in Phoenix, please consider a cash donation. For every dollar that you donate, our food bank can buy $12 worth of food using wholesale discounts. Please mail donations to: Community Food Drive, 2828 N. Central Avenue, Phoenix, AZ 85000. Our success depends on the time and care of our donors and volunteers so thank you all.

The most needed nonperishable foods are:

  • Canned vegetables, fruits, meats, and fish.
  • Pasta and tomato sauce
  • powdered milk
